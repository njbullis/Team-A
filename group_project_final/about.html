Nancy

Nancy is an aspiring web developer who hopes to be widely sought after as an expert in something that revolutionizes an industry. Nancy writes business reference books as well as poetry and fiction. Nancy currently hosts a radio show about writers and the writing process on CIUT 89.5FM.

Erin Cameron

Erin grew up in Ajax, Ontario, where she spent most of her time playing hockey as a child. Her current career is in the healthcare field, working for Toronto Paramedic Services.
